About Snout ’n About

Film people. Animal people. Your kind of people.

Portraits should feel like your pet.

We are a husband-and-wife team with experience in both the film industry and animal care. We bring the storytelling instincts of a film set and the patience real pets need.

No pressure for perfect behavior. We plan carefully, make space for breaks, and follow the moments that feel true. Warm light, expressive details, and genuine connection do the rest.
